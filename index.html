Who am I?


My name is Diego Noria. I am a Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University.
I am an aspiring Game Developer, Software Engineer, Mechanic, Artist, and Musician.
I am currently learning Japanese and have been for the past year. I've slowly become literate, it's been both brutally difficult and wonderful. I hope to live abroad at least once in my life (We'll see if it becomes indefinite). One day I also wish to travel the United States in a heavily modified camper van. I'm hoping it'll force me substantially closer to nature than I currently am. I used to camp quite frequently and I've lost touch with that part of me ever since I aged out of Boyscouts. I am largely self taught in everything I do. I have found school to be quite unproductive for me, so I have developed a very practical perspective on teaching myself topics. I know I'm not alone in this, but learning through projects has taught me much more than purely studying a text book for a test.
